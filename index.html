Welcome to the Parallel and Distributed Systems Laboratory (PADSYS Lab). We are part of the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Florida.

Our research focuses on understanding, modeling, designing, and building real-world computer systems. We work on scalable, efficient, and secure systems for modern computing, with an emphasis on parallel and distributed computing, high-performance communication and I/O, and heterogeneous infrastructures spanning CPUs, GPUs, DPUs, and others. Our recent efforts explore next-generation communication libraries, data movement optimization, and system support for large-scale AI and HPC infrastructures.

We are particularly interested in enabling emerging workloads through co-design across systems, algorithms, and architectures, with strong considerations of performance, scalability, and security. We also collaborate closely with domain scientists to address interdisciplinary challenges in areas such as agriculture, neuroscience, and beyond.

Join Us

We are looking forward to working with self-motivated Ph.D., M.S., and undergraduate students with research interests in parallel and distributed computing systems. Ph.D. students will be supported by full scholarships to cover the tuition fees and living expenses.
